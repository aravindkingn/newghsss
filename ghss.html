Description of Ghss Sirugramam, Panruti, Cuddalore

The location of Ghss Sirugramam is Panruti, Cuddalore. The children get the most out of their education as they are taught by a team of 33 professional and dedicated faculties in this State Board school. Ghss Sirugramam was launched in 1960. This school's primary medium of instruction is Tamil with English as secondary medium and the student teacher ratio is 23:1. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology. Classes from 6 to 12 run in this school. 773 is approximately the current student strength of this school. This school's library has 4600 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too. 100% from this school qualified the examination and out of those, 36% scored first grade with flying colors.
